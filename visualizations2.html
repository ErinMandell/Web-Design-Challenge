Breakdown of Points Scored per Season



This graph is fun because it shows how both the rule change in 1972 on free throws, and the introduction of the 3 point shot in 1980, changed the scoring game.

After 1972, free throws are not taken for non-shooting fouls until after the fifth team foul.It took a number of years for three pointers to become the game changer that they are today, however. Not only did teams need to adjust their game strategy to embrace this new opportunity, but they also needed to get better at making it pay off. Up until 1990, players were less than 20% effective with their three pointers, but player skill consistently improved to ultimately arrive at the 30% we see today.

Note: There was a lockout during the 1998-99 season, so only 50 games were played that year.
