Average Player Height and Age by Year




The average height of NBA players has increased slightly since 1950, from 192 cm to nearly 201 cm in more recent years. Stated in more familiar terms - average height has increased only by 3 inches, which might actually be surprising to some fans.

Average player age remained remarkably constant at around 26 until the late 80's. Most players begin their NBA careers directly out of college at age 22, and the increase in average age reflects the fact that more are able to remain fit and uninjured well into their 30's. An interesting contrast in stats here is that players have enjoyed longer careers since the 80's, while also playing fewer minutes per game. It could be that fewer minutes per season translates directly into fewer injuries per season ...
